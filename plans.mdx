## Upgrade

You can upgrade your plan from the [dashboard](https://lazyraccoon.dev/dashboard) or the [pricing page](https://lazyraccoon.dev/pricing).

## Manage subscription

If you're on a paid plan, you can manage your subscription (cancel, update payment method) from [Settings](https://lazyraccoon.dev/settings).
