---
title: "Roles & Permissions"
description: "Team roles and what each one can do."
---

Every team member has one of three roles: **owner**, **admin**, or **member**.

## Permissions table

| Action | Owner | Admin | Member |
|---|---|---|---|
| View team members | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View team configs | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Push to team configs | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Pull from team configs | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Invite members | Yes | Yes | No |
| Remove members | Yes | Yes* | No |
| Delete team configs | Yes | Yes | No |
| Change member roles | Yes | No | No |
| Rename team | Yes | Yes | No |
| Delete team | Yes | No | No |

<Note>
  *Admins can remove members but cannot remove other admins or owners.
</Note>

## Role details

### Owner

Full control over the team. Can manage roles, delete the team, and perform all actions. An owner **cannot leave** the team — ownership must be transferred first.

A team can have multiple owners.

### Admin

Can manage day-to-day team operations: invite members, manage configs, and remove regular members. Cannot delete the team or change roles.

### Member

Can view the team, push and pull configs. Cannot invite others or manage the team.
